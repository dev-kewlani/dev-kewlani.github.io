Pure Momentum: Behavioral Arbitrage in Cryptocurrency Markets

A quantitative trading strategy exploiting the "Pure Momentum" effect in cryptocurrency markets, where investors systematically respond to changes in reported returns that are unrelated to new information, creating predictable price patterns.

Key Achievements:

  • Developed a data-driven research pipeline to empirically validate the lag-24 effect across multiple cryptocurrencies.
  • Implemented a high-frequency trading strategy that capitalizes on the negative correlation between current returns and lagged-24 hour returns.
  • Created a statistical framework to dynamically identify optimal lag periods and correlation strengths across market conditions.
  • Engineered an hourly rebalancing system with advanced position sizing based on signal strength.
  • Achieved annualized returns of 250%+ with a Sharpe ratio of 3.2 before transaction costs.
  • Analysis showed that spreads on cryptocurrencies (0.5-1% based on the currency) and transaction costs (approximately 1%) significantly reduced profitability, confirming the paper's findings about implementation challenges.
